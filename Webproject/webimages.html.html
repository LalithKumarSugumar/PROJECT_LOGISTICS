Imports and exports are key components of international trade.

Imports are goods and services brought into a country from abroad.

Exports are goods and services sold to other countries.

Countries import items they cannot produce efficiently.

Exports help nations earn foreign exchange and boost their economy.

Balanced trade between imports and exports supports economic stability..

Governments often regulate imports and exports to protect local industries.
